Brading Interface

Global Education Hub is an education‑first directory that organises resources into interface. The platform groups curated links into broad categories, so users can explore relevant services and follow through to external resources. Built in a focused 6‑week design and development sprint, the responsive layout emphasises clear signposting and simple navigation across devices.

For me, design thinking isn’t a rigid checklist — it’s how I approach every project. I start by understanding potential users (what excites them, what frustrates them) and then define the real problem we’re solving . From there, I like to sketch, test, and break ideas quickly, turning concepts into prototypes that I can refine.

I rely on hands-on testing and feedback to push past assumptions, and I always validate the final outcome against both user needs and business goals. This process keeps me grounded in strategy while giving me the freedom to experiment.
